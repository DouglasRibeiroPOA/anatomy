## ü´Ä COMPONENTS OF THE CIRCULATORY SYSTEM

### 1. **Heart**
- Muscular organ located in the thoracic cavity between the lungs.
- Divided into **4 chambers**:
  - **Right Atrium (RA)** ‚Äì receives **deoxygenated blood** from the **body** via the **superior and inferior vena cava**
  - **Right Ventricle (RV)** ‚Äì pumps deoxygenated blood to the **lungs** via the **pulmonary artery**
  - **Left Atrium (LA)** ‚Äì receives **oxygenated blood** from the **lungs** via the **pulmonary veins**
  - **Left Ventricle (LV)** ‚Äì pumps oxygenated blood to the **entire body** via the **aorta**

‚úîÔ∏è The **right atrium** is the **first chamber** to receive **deoxygenated blood**.

### üíì Heart Valves:
| Valve               | Location                                 | Function                                  |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| **Tricuspid**        | Between Right Atrium and Right Ventricle | Prevents backflow into atrium             |
| **Pulmonary Valve**  | Exits Right Ventricle to Pulmonary Artery| Prevents backflow into ventricle          |
| **Bicuspid (Mitral)**| Between Left Atrium and Left Ventricle   | Prevents backflow into atrium             |
| **Aortic Valve**     | Exits Left Ventricle to Aorta            | Prevents backflow into ventricle          |

### üîÅ Circulation Types:
| Type        | Pathway                                 | Purpose                           |
|-------------|------------------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| **Pulmonary** | Heart ‚Üí Lungs ‚Üí Heart                  | Gas exchange (O‚ÇÇ in, CO‚ÇÇ out)      |
| **Systemic**  | Heart ‚Üí Body ‚Üí Heart                   | Supplies tissues with O‚ÇÇ & nutrients |

---

### 2. **Blood Vessels**

| Vessel Type | Function                        | Key Features                            |
|-------------|----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| **Arteries** | Carry blood **away** from heart | **Thick muscular walls**, small lumen   |
| **Arterioles** | Small arteries                  | Regulate blood flow to capillaries      |
| **Capillaries** | Exchange gases, nutrients, waste | **One-cell thick**; connect arteries & veins |
| **Venules** | Small veins                       | Collect blood from capillaries          |
| **Veins**     | Return blood **to** the heart  | **Thin walls**, large lumen, **valves** |

ü©∏ **Blood Flow Path (AACVV)**:  
**A**rteries ‚Üí **A**rterioles ‚Üí **C**apillaries ‚Üí **V**enules ‚Üí **V**eins

‚úîÔ∏è Capillaries arise from **arterioles**

‚úîÔ∏è Arteries = **thick walls, small lumen**  
‚úîÔ∏è Veins = **valves**, prone to **varicose veins** if elasticity is lost

### 3. **Blood**

- **Average volume**: 4‚Äì6 litres
- **Functions**: Transport, protection, regulation

#### üî¥ Components of Blood

| Component               | Function                                     |
|--------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Red Blood Cells (RBCs)** | Transport oxygen via **haemoglobin**        |
| **White Blood Cells (WBCs)** | Fight infection; immune response         |
| **Platelets (Thrombocytes)** | Aid in blood clotting                    |
| **Plasma**               | 90% water; carries hormones, nutrients, waste |

‚úîÔ∏è RBCs = **Erythrocytes** ‚Üí bind O‚ÇÇ with **oxyhaemoglobin**

## ‚ù§Ô∏è BLOOD FLOW THROUGH THE HEART (Step-by-Step)

1. **Deoxygenated blood** enters **Right Atrium** from **superior & inferior vena cava**
2. Blood moves to **Right Ventricle**
3. Pumped to **lungs** via **pulmonary artery**
4. In lungs: picks up O‚ÇÇ, releases CO‚ÇÇ
5. **Oxygenated blood** returns to **Left Atrium** via **pulmonary veins**
6. Moves to **Left Ventricle**
7. Pumped to **body** via **aorta**

üß† **Mnemonic**:  
**RA ‚Üí RV ‚Üí Lungs ‚Üí LA ‚Üí LV ‚Üí Body**

‚úîÔ∏è **Aorta** = major artery carrying oxygenated blood from the heart  
‚úîÔ∏è **Pulmonary artery** = carries **deoxygenated blood**  
‚úîÔ∏è **Pulmonary vein** = carries **oxygenated blood** to the heart

## üí° ITEC QUICK FACTS

- ‚úÖ Arteries = away, veins = toward
- ‚úÖ Pulmonary artery = only artery with deoxygenated blood
- ‚úÖ Pulmonary vein = only vein with oxygenated blood
- ‚úÖ Capillaries arise from arterioles
- ‚úÖ Veins have **valves**; if valves fail ‚Üí **varicose veins**
- ‚úÖ Blood is 55% plasma, 45% cells
- ‚úÖ Red blood cells = **erythrocytes** (oxygen transport)
- ‚úÖ Thick walls + small lumen = **arteries**
- ‚úÖ Haemoglobin in red blood cells binds oxygen as **oxyhaemoglobin**

## ü©∫ CIRCULATORY PATHOLOGIES

| Condition        | Description                                      |
|------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Hypertension** | High blood pressure                              |
| **Atherosclerosis** | Fatty deposits clog arteries                  |
| **Anaemia**      | Low RBC count or haemoglobin                     |
| **Varicose veins** | Weak vein valves; usually in legs              |
| **Heart attack (MI)** | Blocked artery stops blood to heart muscle  |
